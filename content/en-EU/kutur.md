## About the cabin
Kútur is the Skjöldungar scout cabin located on Hellisheiði. The cabin is ideal for scout groups and has sleeping space for 6 people in beds, with several additional mattresses. The cabin includes a kitchenette and dining/table space.

## Price list

Weekend rental: ISK 25,000.

Weekend rental for scouts or other youth activities: ISK 20,000.

Day rental: ISK 12,500.

## Terms of use

Renters are expected to leave the cabin and its surroundings in the same condition as on arrival. If cleaning or tidying is required after the event, the cost will be charged to the renter.
